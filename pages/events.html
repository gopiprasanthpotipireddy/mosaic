FYFP KITAB
From the Youth For the People(FYFP) is a student led,non - profit organization. with the help of our honorable principal sir Dr.K.V.L Raju, collects the old books, used records,other paper waste from all the departments and recycle them to make money for the events they conduct, it was initiated at mvgr by a group of three students in 2010 and started doing different events for the poor and needy in 2012, whose day of incarnation will be celebrated on 11th January every year. From then till now, it has organized different kinds of events like project kitab, project vikas, and organized different social and health campaigns and it is always a helping hand for the needy and poor. It at present has a team of nearly 60 members from all the departments, from all the years in the campus .

• It is one of the most prominent events of the fyfp.

• Kitab, as the name itself indicates it helps the students of primary and upper primary standards of government schools by providing them the note books, copy book and other stationery.

• In this way, it helps the poor students to study by providing them books and stationery by recycling the old books the students of the campus use and other paper waste too.

• This year it collected nearly 6 tonnes of paper waste (all the year long) and helped nearly 800 students of nearby government schools in vizianagaram district with the books they require.

• Not only a help for the students but also it helps the college to become a green campus as it recycles the waste paper which is eco- friendly.

• Moreover, it inculcates in the students the motto of service, and makes them leaders of the future.
